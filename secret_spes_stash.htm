Air Quality

Air Quality Spreadsheet: Last updated November 2017 I grabbed publicly-available data from provincial websites and graphed out changes in the examined indicators (i.e. various chemical indicators in the air and the Air Quality Health Index) as Vanessa wanted to see if there could be any relationship between the Park's biodiversity and common air quality health indicators.
R Code: Last updated January 2018 You'll also need airquality2.csv (last updated November 2017). Yukon() is the R function I wrote that was based off of what the Yukon people used as an indicator: how many days was the AQHI above a certain threshold they decided. I had no idea what threshold you would want, so yukon() is a quick-fire way to chart the temporal trends of the number of days where the AQHI is above the threshold your after.

Sea Level

R Code: Last updated November 2017 You'll also need deep4.csv (last updated November 2017), deep5.csv (last updated November 2017), and deepest2.csv (last updated November 2017) for this to work.
Several functions are in this file: watermonth() is there to chart trends in sea level based on monthly averages. watermonthloop() is a quick way to plot out the parameters of the linear regression over time based on monthly averages. wateryear() is there to chart trends in sea level based on yearly averages. wateryearloop() is a quick way to plot out the parameters of the linear regression over time based on monthly averages.
Also available are some notes on sea level rise in Vancouver and British Columbia that I gathered (last updated November 2017).
